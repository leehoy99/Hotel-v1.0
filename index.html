Stay

Our hotels are open 24/7 for overnight stays. The locker and capsule areas are completely separated for men and women at the entrance. Before or after checking in, you're welcome to leave your luggage with us to make your stay more convenient. Discount plans are available on a first-come, first-served basis, so be sure to make your bookings early.

Nap

Our hotels can also be used for a few hours during the day as a resting place Rates start at 1,000 yen for the first hour plus an additional 500 yen for each hour afterwards. Both beds and showers are available for use. This service can be used as much as you like between the hours of 14:00 and 21:00.

Shower

We also offer access to only showers, which can be used for up to an hour at a cost of 700 yen. Towels, shampoo, conditioner, body soap, and other amenities are provided, so you’re welcome to come empty-handed. Showers are available 24 hours a day, so feel free to use them to freshen up before or after work or during your long days of sightseeing around the city.
